# Permit Fee Intelligence - Quick Start Guide

## For End Users

### Using Fee Intelligence in Your Permit Workflow

#### Method 1: Check Fees While Creating Permit Drawings
1. Open **Permit Drawing Wizard**
2. Complete all project specification steps
3. Reach the **"Review"** step (Step 6)
4. Click the green **"Fee Check"** button
5. Enter your project details:
   - Street address
   - ZIP code
   - City/Municipality
   - County
   - Estimated project value (optional)
6. Click **"Search Official Sources"**
7. Wait 15-30 seconds for AI search
8. Review detected fees in results:
   - Green badge = High confidence
   - Yellow badge = Medium confidence  
   - Gray badge = Low confidence
9. Close the dialog (fees are noted for future reference)

#### Method 2: Check Fees While Creating a Bid
1. Open **Bid Wizard**
2. Complete **Basics** step (Step 1)
3. Complete **Costs** step (Step 2)
4. Reach **"Permit Fees"** step (Step 3)
5. Click **"Run Fee Intelligence"**
6. Enter project details and search
7. Review and confirm fees
8. Check the boxes for fees you want to include
9. Fees automatically add to your bid total
10. Continue with remaining bid steps

### Understanding Fee Information

Each fee shows:
- **Fee Name** - What the fee is for (e.g., "Building Permit")
- **Amount** - Dollar estimate (may vary based on project details)
- **Confidence** - How certain the AI is about this fee
- **Source** - Which government office provided this info
- **Dependencies** - If fee depends on project value or size
- **Notes** - Additional information

### Tips for Accurate Fees

✓ **DO:**
- Include estimated project value if known
- Provide complete address with ZIP code
- Use accurate project type
- Always call building department to confirm before submitting bid
- Ask about additional optional fees
- Verify current fees (they change annually)

✗ **DON'T:**
- Rely entirely on estimates without verification
- Assume all fees shown are required
- Use estimates without calling to confirm
- Forget to include fees in your bid
- Submit a bid without confirming final fee amounts

## For Contractors/Bid Creators

### Integrating Fees Into Your Bid

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Create bid normally (Basics → Costs)
2. Reach Permit Fees step
3. Run Fee Intelligence or manually add known fees
4. Review the "Total Permit-Related Fees" box
5. This amount automatically adds to your direct costs
6. Your bid total, overhead, and margins adjust automatically
7. Each fee appears as its own line item in bid summary

### Manual Fee Entry

If you already know permit fees:
1. In Permit Fees step, find "Add Manual Fee"
2. Enter fee name (e.g., "Zoning Variance Fee")
3. Enter amount
4. Click "Add"
5. Fee now included in calculations

### Removing Fees

1. If you don't want a detected fee, uncheck its checkbox
2. Or click the trash icon to remove it completely
3. Bid total automatically recalculates

### What Gets Included in Final Bid

✓ **Included:**
- All checked/selected permit fees
- Appear as separate line items
- Add to direct costs calculation
- Affect profit margins and final bid amount

✗ **Not included:**
- Unchecked fees
- Removed fees

## Important: Always Verify!

### Before Submitting a Bid:

1. **Call the building department** with:
   - Full project address
   - Project type (deck, roof, etc.)
   - Estimated project value
   - Scope of work

2. **Ask for:**
   - Current permit fee amounts
   - All applicable fees for your project
   - Any conditional or discretionary fees
   - Whether fees depend on square footage or value
   - Payment timing and methods
   - Processing timeline

3. **Update your bid if:**
   - Actual fees differ from estimates
   - Additional fees apply
   - Fee amounts have changed
   - Building department identified optional fees

### Disclaimer

*Permit fees shown are AI-assisted estimates based on official public information. These estimates may change without notice. Final fees must be verified directly with your local building and zoning office before submitting to clients.*

## Troubleshooting

### No Fees Found
- Your jurisdiction may not have published online fee schedules
- Try entering different address details
- Call building department for manual fee inquiry
- You can add fees manually

### Fees Seem Low/High
- Some fees are percentage-based (not shown in estimates)
- Some fees depend on project specifics not captured
- Always verify with building department
- Fees may have changed since last update

### AI Search Times Out
- Internet search may be slow
- Try searching again
- If persistent, add fees manually
- Contact building department for confirmed amounts

## Key Reminders

1. **Estimates ≠ Final Amounts**
   - Always confirm with building department
   - Fee schedules change annually
   - Some fees depend on factors not captured

2. **Accuracy Matters**
   - Forgotten permit fees can hurt profit
   - Overestimated fees lose bids
   - Verification takes 10 minutes
   - Worth the time investment

3. **Professional Practice**
   - Document your fee research
   - Keep building department contact info
   - Note when you verified fees
   - Reference confirmation in bid file
